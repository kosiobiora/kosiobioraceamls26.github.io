  Today's session began with the completion of a pre survey designed to gather information about our experiences with academic writing. Afterward, we were split into breakout rooms, where I met with my faculty and graduate mentors to discuss the progress of our research project. During this meeting, we continued exploring the dataset we will be working with and shared our individual observations, insights, and questions about the data. We also discussed how these observations could influence the direction of our research moving forward. Following this, Dr. Anita Pandey led a session on literature reviews, where she explained their role in research and shared strategies for critically analyzing academic sources. During the session, she also introduced us to the PEACC checklist for papers and presentations, which emphasizes Professionalism, Ethicality, Accuracy, Clarity, and Completeness. After lunch, we participated in an engaging Amazon Careers activity in the form of a Kahoot game, which introduced us to different career pathways and opportunities within the company Amazon. The final activity of the day was our Python 101 session, where we learned about functions and their importance in writing and reusing code. We then applied these concepts through practice exercises, which helped strengthen my understanding of how functions work.
